Sealed Secrets deployed.

시크릿 추가/수정 방법:
  1. kubeseal --fetch-cert --controller-name=sealed-secrets --controller-namespace=kube-system > pub-cert.pem
  2. kubectl create secret generic my-secret --from-literal=key=value --dry-run=client -o yaml | kubeseal --cert pub-cert.pem -o yaml > templates/my-secret.yaml
  3. git commit & push → ArgoCD sync
